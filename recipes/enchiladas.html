Enchiladas

Description

Cheese enchiladas are a delicious and comforting Mexican dish that is beloved by many. These enchiladas typically consist of soft corn tortillas that are rolled around a filling of melted cheese, creating a delightful combination of flavors and textures. The enchiladas are then smothered in a rich and flavorful tomato-based sauce, such as red enchilada sauce, which further enhances their taste. Topped with a sprinkle of cheese and garnished with fresh cilantro, these cheese enchiladas are a mouthwatering treat that will satisfy any cheese lover's cravings.

One bite of cheese enchiladas reveals the harmonious blend of ingredients that make them a crowd-pleasing favorite. The warm and gooey cheese filling provides a luscious and melty contrast to the softness of the corn tortillas. The tangy and savory red enchilada sauce adds a depth of flavor, complementing the cheese perfectly. With each bite, the combination of the tender tortillas, the cheesy interior, and the savory sauce creates a symphony of tastes that will leave you wanting more. Whether enjoyed as a main course or a side dish, cheese enchiladas are a classic and delightful treat that will transport your taste buds to the vibrant flavors of Mexico.

Ingredients

  • Cheddar or Mexican Cheese
  • Flour or corn tortillas
  • Enchilada sauce
  • Black or pinto beans

Steps

  1. Heat sauce and beans
  2. Spread beans in tortillas
  3. Roll tortillas
  4. Top with cheese and sauce
  5. Heat at 350 degrees for 15 minutes
  6. Serve with additional sauce, sour cream, and guacomole
