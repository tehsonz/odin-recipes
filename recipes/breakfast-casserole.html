Breakfast Casserole

Description

Breakfast casserole is a hearty and satisfying dish that combines various breakfast ingredients into a flavorful and convenient one-pan meal. It typically consists of a base of eggs, mixed with ingredients such as cooked bacon or sausage, diced vegetables like onions and bell peppers, and grated cheese. The mixture is then baked until golden and set, resulting in a deliciously savory and filling breakfast option. Breakfast casserole is not only delicious, but it's also incredibly versatile, allowing for endless variations to suit personal preferences. Whether it's a classic combination of ham and cheese or a vegetarian-friendly medley of spinach and feta, breakfast casserole provides a convenient and delicious way to start the day.

A breakfast casserole is a fantastic option for those looking for a make-ahead meal that can be easily reheated for busy mornings. By assembling the casserole the night before and refrigerating it overnight, all you need to do in the morning is pop it in the oven for a warm and satisfying breakfast. The beauty of breakfast casserole lies in its ability to be customized to individual tastes and dietary needs. From incorporating gluten-free ingredients to adding a variety of herbs and spices, the possibilities are endless. Whether you're feeding a crowd or simply want a comforting and flavorful breakfast for yourself, breakfast casserole is a versatile and delicious choice that provides a convenient and enjoyable start to the day.

Ingredients

  • 1lb Italian sausage
  • 1 C Veggies
  • 2 C Cheese
  • 12 Eggs
  • 1/2 tbsp Milk
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350. Grease 9x13 pan.
  2. Cook sausage on stovetop
  3. Mix eggs, milk, salt and pepper
  4. Combine sausage and veggies in pan
  5. Pour egg mixture over mixture
  6. Top with cheese
  7. Bake for 1 hour
